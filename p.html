About Us

We've ben making healthy and tasty food last for 20 years

Vindu is a new place in Guntur, founded by chefs Noah and Gnan. They finally managed to open their first Indian restaurant after living in US for the last 8 years. Vindu is inspired by the small Indian brasseries that can be found in every village in India where the food is tasty, authentic,fresh and delicious. The two chefs also decided to run a small catering company and help local event planners have a taste of India on every occasions.
